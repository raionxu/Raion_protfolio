A Touch of Memory

- An immersive Virtual reality project based on hand interaction -

-

Concept

The work is presented in a VR environment, where the scene models are composed using 3D scanning technology to represent the appearance of my "home" from my personal memory.

Only the area around the hands will be visible in the space, and players will explore objects and space in the darkness through touch-based interactions with their hands.

The piece explores the theme of memory while also investigating the relationship between the body and digital space.
