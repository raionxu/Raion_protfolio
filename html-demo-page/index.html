- A Video Installation Based on Camera Interaction -

Concept

This work discuss the relationship between observing and being observed Use P5js to create an virtual exhibition hall,

where audience will see their own image after entering the interaction area.

The art work exists only when it is "observed" by the viewer.

When the audience looks at the artwork in front of them, they also become part of the artwork.
